Welcome to BITTER + SWEET.
We are a local coffeeshop based in Cupertino, CA, just off S. De Anza Blvd.
We serve the best coffee and desserts in the South Bay Community.

We stand proudly behind our products and service, and invite you in to be treated to an unforgettable experience. We have tea, chai lattes, and a white matcha latte for those who prefer non-coffee drinks. And if you are in the mood for espresso and ice cream, we do a great affogato with Tahitian Vanilla ice cream.

We are located at 20560 TOWN CENTER LANE, in VILLAGIO CENTER,
just a 2 minutes' walk from the CUPERTINO LIBRARY and CITY HALL.
Our neighboring retail tenants include Arirang Tofu and BBQ, Curry House,
and Kee Wah Bakery.
